Our Mission

Here at EVE…

we Empower women to express their femininity with confidence, we Value women for their individuality, and we Elevate women's lifestyles through exposure to new opportunities.

EVE welcomes all women regardless of age, race, religion, ethnicity, nationality, ability, or sexual orientation. We chose the name EVE to embrace everything created in the feminine energy while simultaneously challenging rigid definitions of what it means to be a woman.

Empower

To express femininity with confidence — and to move through the world unapologetically ourselves.

Value

Women for their individuality — honoring every story, background, and way of being a woman.

Elevate

Women's lifestyles through exposure to new opportunities, spaces, and connections.
About Us

A community rooted
in feminine energy

EVE is a women's community brand built to gather women and femme-expressing individuals into spaces where they can learn, restore, and rise. We believe growth is gentler — and lasts longer — when it happens in good company.

Every gathering is designed around one of our three core experiences, so there's always a way in — whether you come to learn, to be pampered, or to build your next opportunity.

Three core experiences

Every EVE event is an invitation into one of these.

Learn & Grow

Hands-on workshops, thoughtful guides, and honest community discussion — spaces to expand your skills, your perspective, and your sense of what's possible. The toothpaste-making gathering is our first of many.

Luxury & Pampering

Curated experiences that indulge, soften, and restore — because rest and beauty are not extras, they're part of how we honor ourselves.

Career & Networking

Connections, mentorship, and opportunities that elevate the path you're already on — and open doors to the ones you haven't seen yet.

The women behind EVE

Meet the founders

Founder

Joslyn René Harper

“I have always had a passion for uplifting those around me. I pride myself on being a person who makes others feel comfortable being who they are. I wear the term ‘hype-woman’ as a badge of honor! I love myself and the life I have been blessed with. I hope to inspire every person I come across to reach the same love I have.”

Joslyn René grew up in the suburbs of Sacramento in a family of six. She was an honor roll student and competitive athlete throughout her entire childhood. She went on to study at Santa Clara University, graduating with a B.S. in Biology. During her time at Santa Clara, she was very involved on campus, often working two or more jobs at once, and completed a research project that resulted in a publication.

Since graduating in 2020, she has worked in a few biotech labs in the San Francisco area. Now Joslyn René is focusing on creating her own path and building the life that feels right for her. She currently works as a desk assistant for a local gym, is a petsitter on Rover, and works for a jewelry retail store. She is the founder of EVE and the OwnIt app.

Joslyn René lives in San Francisco with her sweet, fluffy cat ShooShoo. In her free time, she can be found enjoying life with her friends, dancing tango, or planning her next vacation.
